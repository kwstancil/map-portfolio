Global Covid Vaccine Apartheid

This map depicts unequal access to Covid-19 vaccines around the world and also highlights which countries support an initiative to temporarily suspend intellectual property restrictions to boost the production of life-saving doses.

It was generated in Carto using GeoJSON files containing national inoculation rates (courtesy of Our World in Data) as well as governments' positions on the TRIPS waiver proposal under negotiation at the WTO (courtesy of Doctors Without Borders).

Patterns of Economic Hardship in the U.S.

This map documents the poverty rate in each U.S. county.

It was generated in Leaflet with a GeoJson file produced by joining 2019 data from the U.S. Census Bureau's Small Area Income and Poverty Estimates (SAIPE) Program with U.S. county polygons.

Renting in the USA

This map shows average monthly rent prices in every county nationwide.

It was generated in Leaflet using a GeoJson file produced by joining 2015 data from the U.S. Census Bureau with U.S. county polygons.

Housing Patterns in Kentucky

This map portrays a variety of housing patterns across Kentucky, including the percentage of renters, mortgagors, outright owners, and vacant units in each county.

It was generated in Leaflet using a GeoJson file produced by joining 2010 data from the U.S. Census Bureau with Kentucky county polygons.

Renewable Energy Production in the U.S.

This map illustrates how many megawatts of electricty are generated by solar farms, wind farms, dams, geothermal power plants, and nuclear power plants in the U.S.

It was generated in Leaflet using 2021 data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA).

About me

Hi, I'm Kenny. Born and raised in Denver, I moved to Tacoma for college and graduated from Pacific Lutheran University in 2013 with a degree in political science and global studies. I then pursued graduate study in geography and earned an MA in secondary social studies education from the University of Kentucky. In 2020, after a few years of teaching civics and history at a public high school and working as a GIS analyst in Lexington, I joined the newsroom at Common Dreams, where I'm a staff writer.

When I'm not writing or making maps, I enjoy spending time outdoors and keeping up with my beloved Colorado sports teams.
